ABOUT IFIS MODEL-PLUS
The IFIS-Model Plus is a web platform developed by the Iowa Flood Center (IFC) for the visualization of flood-related modelled data. It is built using the Iowa Flood Information System (IFIS) and is initially aimed to provide an user-friendly interface for outputs of the hydrological models being executed in real time by the IFC for the State of Iowa and contributing watersheds.

This platform provides access to animated maps of different components of the hydrological cycle such as accumulated precipitation, accumulated generated runoff, pounded water, surface soil moisture, total subsurface soil water column, streamflow classification based on estimated recurrence interval and flood magnitude estimation. Such maps can be visualized individually or in specific compositions for both single models results and for comparison of models outputs.

Additional functionalities include the execution of simulations for historical rainfall events present in the IFC data repository, such as the large-scale flood-generating events of 2008 and 2016 in Iowa.
